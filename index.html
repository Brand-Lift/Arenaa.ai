About Our Clinic

Where Expert Care Meets Modern Dentistry

Prabhat Dental Clinic has been a cornerstone of trusted dental care on Kalwar Road, Radha Colony. Founded with a mission to make high-quality dental treatment accessible and comfortable, our clinic combines modern technology with a warm, patient-first approach.

Under the expert guidance of Dr. BL Degra, we provide a full spectrum of dental services — from routine cleanings to complex restorative procedures — all in a clean, hygienic, and welcoming environment.

  • Certified and experienced dental professionals
  • Sterile, hygienic and modern facility
  • Transparent treatment plans and honest pricing

Our Services

Comprehensive Dental Treatments

From routine care to advanced procedures, we offer everything your smile needs under one roof.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional scaling and polishing to remove plaque, tartar, and surface stains for a brighter smile.

Root Canal Treatment

Pain-free procedures to save infected teeth and restore full function using modern techniques.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacements that restore your smile and confidence for life.

Braces / Aligners

Corrective orthodontic solutions including traditional braces and clear aligners for aligned teeth.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Smile makeovers, teeth whitening, veneers and cosmetic procedures for your perfect smile.

Tooth Extraction

Safe, gentle extractions performed with modern anaesthesia for a pain-free experience.
